Questions

COMMON QUESTIONS

Is the strategy call really free?
Yes — completely free, zero obligation. The call is 15 minutes with Coach Emman. He reviews your application, tells you what's been holding you back, and outlines what a personalized plan would look like for you. You decide afterwards if you want to move forward — no pressure at all.
I'm a complete beginner. Will this work for me?
Absolutely. EP Fitness works with clients of all experience levels — from people who have never touched a weight to competitive athletes. Your program is built specifically for where you are right now, not some assumed fitness baseline. Beginners often see the fastest results.
I don't have a gym. Can I still train with EP Fitness?
Yes. Programs can be built for commercial gyms, home gyms, condo gyms, or even minimal equipment setups. Just mention your situation in the application and Coach Emman will build around what you have access to.
How is this different from just following a YouTube workout?
YouTube workouts are generic — built for nobody in particular. EP Fitness builds a program specifically for your body, goals, schedule, and available equipment. You also get real coach accountability — someone checking your form, tracking your numbers, and adjusting your plan when you plateau. That's the difference between guessing and having a system.
What's included in the free nutrition assessment?
Every EP Fitness client receives a complimentary nutrition assessment from Coach EJ Patricio, a Registered Nutritionist Dietitian. This covers your current dietary habits, your goals, and initial personalized recommendations — not just generic advice. Full monthly nutrition coaching packages are also available.
How long before I see results?
Most clients notice physical changes within 3–4 weeks. Significant visible results typically appear by weeks 8–12 depending on your starting point, consistency, and goal. Coach Emman sets realistic timelines during your strategy call so you know exactly what to expect and when.
What if it doesn't work for me?
EP Fitness offers a 7-day money-back guarantee on all coaching packages. If you follow the program and are not satisfied within the first 7 days, contact either coach for a full refund — no questions asked. The risk is entirely on us.
